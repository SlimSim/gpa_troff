Tips for Troff

SpacePlay/pause (with countdown, from marker)
ReturnPlay/pause (without countdown)
EscStop song
M Set Marker
I Edit song info
E Edit selected markers info
1-9Set number of times to loop song
0 Loop ''Infinit'' (∞) many times
P Toggle ''Pause bef start''
B Toggle ''Start Before''
A Toggle ''Stopp After''
F Toggle ''Play in fullscreen''-mode
U Play the entire (full) song
T Tapp repeatedly to calculate tempo of song
V Set Volume to 100%
S Set Speed to 100%
W Set Wait to 1 second
  • Use space or enter, as primary button.
  • Select the name of a marker and "Stop" of another marker to only play a part of the song.
  • Use Loop "∞" (or 0) to loop that part of a song,
    press a number in the Loop square to loop that that many times (or use the numbers on the keyboard) .
  • Use "pause bef start" (or P) to control how long the countdown is before music starts to play.
    use "wait" to control countdown between loops
  • Use "start before" or "stop after" (B and A) to start play the music before or after a marker.
  • Use 'Shift/Alt'+ P/B/A/W to increase or decrease their respective value
  • Every square is a button; try to press them and see what happens!
  • Press "Volume:" (or V) to set volume to 100%.
    Press "Speed:" (or S) to set speed to 100%.
  • Use 'Shift/Alt'+ V/S to increase or decrease Volume/Speed
  • Use 'Shift/Alt'+ U to select the start-marker or stop-marker respectively
  • Songs and markers are automatically saved next time you enter Troff.
  • Troff supports movies, simply add a folder containing a movie and you're good to go. If you toggle ''Play in Fullscreen'' on, the video will enter fullscreen as soon as you starts playing, and exit automatically when the video is paused.
  • You can export your markers, including the markerinfo, to let other people use your markers and comments. Simply press "I/E" and then "Export Markers". You will then get a text-string that represents the markers for the current song - copy this text-string and give it to the persons that needs the markers.
  • You can also import markers from some one else's Troff. Press "I/E" and then "Import Markers". Paste the exact string you've received and all the markers will be added to the current song.
  • The Song info-text area is an area for longer notes specific to each song. It allowes for rowbreaks and is perfect for detailed explanation of how the song should be preformed, for future reference. Click the text or press I to start edit the song info area, and press Ctrl + Enter or click outside the marker info area to stop edditing.
  • The Marker info-text area is an area for longer notes, which will be saved per marker. It allowes for rowbreaks and is perfect for detailed explanation of what to do at that specific marker, for future reference. Click the text or press E to edit the marker info area, and press Ctrl + Enter or click outside the marker info area to stop edditing.
